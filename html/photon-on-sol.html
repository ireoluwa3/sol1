Photon on SOL

Please visit https://photon-sol.tinyastro.io/ and connect with Phantom wallet

After you connect a wallet to Photon and login for the first time, you'll be greeted with the above welcome screen. Once you click on the Generate button, a new wallet will be generated for you.

To ensure that we keep your account as secure as possible, the private key for your Photon trading wallet is only revealed to you only once. You'll need to use the slider to reveal the private key. PLEASE NOTE: Your private key will be revealed ONLY ONCE at this step. Please take a moment to save your private key in a safe place before clicking "Continue."

From here, you can deposit SOL into your new Photon trading wallet and click on Check Balance to see the updated balance. Click on Continue to get started on the Photon Platform! Wallet Balance + Transfer Funds

You can see your wallet balance at the upper right-hand corner at all times, and you can click on it to get to the Funds Transfer page.

Deposit: You can specify any SOL amount to deposit from your funding wallet to your Photon trading wallet. Once you click "Deposit", you'll be able to confirm and complete the transaction from your funding wallet.

Withdraw: Input the amount of SOL and the wallet address that you'd like to withdraw the SOL to, and click on "Withdraw"
